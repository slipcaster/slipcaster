slipcaster — plaster from polygons

Model. Print. Pour.

You shape a vessel in 3D. A vase, a cup, a teapot, whatever you want to make more than one of. Slipcaster figures out the rest.

Print the pieces, cast plaster into them, and you've got a working mold.
